Mutual Exclusions

  • Armor

    Protection Types

    Protection, Fire Prot, Blast Prot, and Projectile Prot cannot be on the same piece.

  • Tools

    Silk Touch vs Fortune

    You must choose between getting the block or getting more items.

  • Combat

    Melee Damage Types

    Sharpness, Smite, and Bane of Arthropods are mutually exclusive.

  • Bow

    Mending vs Infinity

    Self-repairing vs Unlimited arrows. Most choose Infinity for bows.

Special Exclusions

  • Mace

    Density vs Breach

    Density (Raw Damage) vs Breach (Armor Pen). Mutually exclusive.

  • Trident

    Riptide Restrictions

    Riptide is incompatible with Loyalty and Channeling.

  • Boots

    Water Movement

    Frost Walker and Depth Strider cannot be combined.

Wolves

  • Food: Bones (multiple may be needed).
  • Behavior: Tamed wolves follow you, attack hostile mobs, and wear a red collar.
  • Control: Interact to make them sit. Sitting wolves stay put.

Cats

  • Food: Raw Salmon or Raw Cod (Crouch-walk to approach).
  • Perks: Deters Creepers and Phantoms. May bring gifts after you sleep.
  • Control: Interact to make them sit. Wear a red collar when tamed.

Horses & Donkeys

  • Taming: Repeatedly ride them until hearts appear.
  • Equip: Use a **Saddle** to control. Horses take armor; Donkeys/Mules take chests.
  • Special: Skeleton horses are tamed by killing the original rider.

Llamas

  • Taming: Ride repeatedly until hearts appear.
  • Equip: Can be equipped with a chest. No saddle allowed.
  • Behavior: Use a lead on one to form a caravan with others.

Parrots

  • Food: Any Seeds. WARNING: Cookies are poisonous!
  • Behavior: Perches on shoulders, mimics nearby mob sounds.
  • Control: Interact to sit/follow.

Nautiluses (April Fools)

  • Food: Pufferfish or Pufferfish Buckets.
  • Riding: Requires a **Saddle** to direct. Hold jump for a long dash.
  • Ability: Share their underwater breathing while riding.

Ghastlings (April Fools)

  • Tame/Growth: Tempt/Feed with **Snowballs**.
  • Happy Ghast: Rideable with a **Harness**. Supports up to 4 players!
  • Driver: The first player to mount gets to drive the ghast.
