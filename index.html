Mutual Exclusions

  • Armor

    Protection Types

    Protection, Fire Prot, Blast Prot, and Projectile Prot cannot be on the same piece.

  • Tools

    Silk Touch vs Fortune

    You must choose between getting the block or getting more items.

  • Combat

    Melee Damage Types

    Sharpness, Smite, and Bane of Arthropods are mutually exclusive.

  • Bow

    Mending vs Infinity

    Self-repairing vs Unlimited arrows. Most choose Infinity for bows.

Special Exclusions

  • Mace

    Density vs Breach

    Density (Raw Damage) vs Breach (Armor Pen). Mutually exclusive.

  • Trident

    Riptide Restrictions

    Riptide is incompatible with Loyalty and Channeling.

  • Boots

    Water Movement

    Frost Walker and Depth Strider cannot be combined.

Golden Dandelion

Craft with 1 Dandelion and 8 Gold Nuggets. Feeding this to a baby mob stops its aging process permanently. Feed again to resume growth.

Craftable Name Tags

Combine 1 Paper and 1 Metal Nugget (Iron, Gold, or Copper) in a crafting table. Essential for the new permanent baby mobs!

Wolf Armor

Crafted from Armadillo Scutes. Protects your wolf from almost all damage types. Can be dyed and repaired while on the wolf.

Copper Note Blocks

Place any Copper Block under a Note Block for a Trumpet sound. Pitch varies with oxidation!

Recovery Compass

Points to the location of your last death. Essential for retrieving gear in the Nether or Caves.

Totem of Undying

Prevents death if held in hand. Grants Regeneration II (45s), Absorption II (5s), and Fire Resistance I (40s).

Wind Charge

Launches entities and player. Use to double-jump or knock back mobs. Negates fall damage if used at the feet.

Bundle

Consolidates up to 64 different items into 1 slot. Perfect for exploring and gathering multiple low-stack items.
